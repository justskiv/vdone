## Theme

[<img src="https://github-readme-stats.vercel.app/api/pin/?username=xugaoyi&amp;repo=vuepress-theme-vdoing" alt="ReadMe Card" class="no-zoom">](https://github.com/xugaoyi/vuepress-theme-vdoing)

This site uses the [`Vdoing`](https://github.com/xugaoyi/vuepress-theme-vdoing) theme, a clean and efficient VuePress knowledge management & blog theme. It's designed to easily build a personal online knowledge base & blog that combines `structured` and `fragmented` content, making your knowledge ocean as clear and readable as a collection of books. Combined with multi-dimensional indexing, every knowledge point can be quickly located!
