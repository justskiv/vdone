Have you ever noticed that the most capable and knowledgeable people around us tend to be the most humble and modest? That's because they deeply understand that the more you know, the more you realize how much you don't know.
<!-- more -->

"The more you know, the more you don't know" is a profoundly philosophical statement.

Everyone's knowledge has limits. You might have deep expertise in a particular field and become a specialist, but once you reach the top, you realize just how small you are — how many domains remain unexplored. The more you know, the more questions, doubts, and uncertainties arise about what you already know.

Even so, we should keep striving. At the very least, we can become outstanding in some field.

::: tip Inspiration 1
Weak people tend to mock and dismiss others, while those who are truly strong never hesitate to praise and encourage.
:::

::: tip Inspiration 2
Young people today should rise above the cynicism. Just keep moving upward — don't listen to the naysayers. Do what you can, speak when you can. If you have warmth, give off light — like a firefly that can still shine in the darkness. You don't have to wait for the torch.
:::
